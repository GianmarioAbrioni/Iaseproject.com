Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how IASE Project uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience and provide analytics on website usage.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., login, security).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Enhance user experience (e.g., remembering preferences).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads (if applicable).

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact site functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may use cookies from third-party services (e.g., embedded videos, analytics tools). These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of those services.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at: contact@iaseproject.com.
